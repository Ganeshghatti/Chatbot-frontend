Frequently Asked Questions

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan later?

Yes, you can easily upgrade or downgrade your subscription plan at any time. Changes will be applied to your next billing cycle.

What happens after the free month?

After your free month, you'll be automatically subscribed to the plan you selected. We'll send you a reminder email 3 days before your subscription begins.

Do I own my chatbot data?

Absolutely. All the data you provide and the conversations your chatbot has with customers remain your property. We never sell or share your data with third parties.

How long does it take to set up a custom chatbot?

Development time for custom chatbots varies based on complexity. Basic solutions take around 2 weeks, while more advanced implementations can take 3-6 weeks.

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our chatbot solutions and services.

Our ready-made chatbot solutions come with a free 1-month trial. During this period, we'll set up the chatbot with your company data and customize it to your basic needs. You'll have full access to all features without any payment required. After the trial period, you'll be automatically subscribed to the plan you selected unless you cancel. We'll send you a reminder email 3 days before your subscription begins.

Ready-made chatbots are pre-built solutions that can be quickly deployed and customized with your company data. They're ideal for standard customer service needs, FAQs, and basic interactions. These come with a free month trial and then switch to a subscription model.

Custom chatbots are built from scratch specifically for your business needs. They include advanced features, complex integrations, and unique conversation flows designed around your specific processes. These require a one-time development fee plus ongoing maintenance.

Implementation time varies depending on the solution:

  • Ready-made chatbots can be deployed in as little as 15 minutes for basic setups, or up to 48 hours if more extensive data integration is needed.
  • Custom chatbots typically take 2-4 weeks from initial consultation to deployment, depending on complexity and specific requirements.

Yes, you can easily upgrade or downgrade your subscription plan at any time through your client dashboard. Changes to your subscription will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle. If you upgrade mid-cycle, we'll prorate the difference.

Integration is simple. Once your chatbot is ready, we'll provide you with a small JavaScript code snippet to add to your website. Just copy and paste this code into your site's HTML, and the chatbot will automatically appear. We also offer integration options for popular platforms like WordPress, Shopify, and Wix with plug-and-play solutions.

To customize your chatbot, we typically need:

  • Basic company information (name, services, hours, etc.)
  • Frequently asked questions and their answers
  • Product or service information
  • Common customer queries and preferred responses
  • Any specific workflows you want the chatbot to handle

We'll guide you through the process and provide templates to make data collection easy.

Yes, our Professional and Enterprise subscription plans support multi-language capabilities. The chatbot can detect the user's language and respond accordingly. We currently support over 30 languages, including English, Spanish, French, German, Chinese, Japanese, and Arabic. Custom chatbots can be built with language-specific features tailored to your target markets.

We take data security and privacy very seriously. All data is encrypted both in transit and at rest using industry-standard encryption protocols. We're GDPR compliant and can provide HIPAA-compliant solutions for healthcare clients. We maintain strict access controls, regular security audits, and never sell or share your data with third parties. You retain full ownership of all your data and customer conversations.

Yes, our chatbots can integrate with a wide range of existing systems and platforms including:

  • CRM systems (Salesforce, HubSpot, Zoho, etc.)
  • E-commerce platforms (Shopify, WooCommerce, Magento)
  • Helpdesk software (Zendesk, Freshdesk, Help Scout)
  • Calendar systems (Google Calendar, Outlook, Calendly)
  • Payment processors (Stripe, PayPal, Square)
  • Custom APIs and databases

Custom chatbots offer more extensive integration options than ready-made solutions.

Our support options vary by plan:

  • Basic Plan: Email support with 24-48 hour response time, knowledge base access
  • Professional Plan: Priority email support with 12-24 hour response time, knowledge base access, monthly check-ins
  • Enterprise Plan: 24/7 email and phone support, dedicated account manager, knowledge base access, bi-weekly check-ins
  • Custom Chatbots: Dedicated support team, implementation assistance, and training sessions

All plans include access to our comprehensive documentation and video tutorials.
